My sessions are 30 minutes long and run Wednesday through Friday at 1:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m., with an additional session Saturday morning at 11:00 a.m. Each session is $21. Please arrive 10 minutes early, as late arrivals cannot be accommodated.

I’m honored to serve you on your healing journey.

How I Became Aware of My Power

In 2020, while living in New York, I experienced something I couldn’t explain. I went to bed one evening with perfect fingernails. The next morning, every one of them had cracked in half. As they healed, I had to wear gloves to protect them. Looking back, I now recognize that moment as the beginning of my healing journey.

My first healing was a distance healing. A friend in Virginia called me after suffering a severe allergic reaction to a cream prescribed by her dermatologist. The reaction was so intense that she had been unable to work for a week.

As I listened to her, I felt guided to help. I began channeling healing frequencies from New York to Virginia. She immediately told me she could feel the energy, and within a short time she experienced such significant relief that she felt ready to return to work the following day.

I was amazed. It was my very first healing experience, and it confirmed that something extraordinary had taken place. Since then, I have come to innerstand that I am simply a vessel through which healing frequencies flow—from Source, through me, to those who are open to receiving them. I believe each person is an active participant in their own healing. My role is to facilitate the flow of these frequencies; your role is to receive them with an open heart and mind, allowing them to support your highest good.
